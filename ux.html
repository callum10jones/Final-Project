Flowchart

A website flowchart can be used as a planning tool to help manage and clarify existing content while also removing redundant or unnecessary content. Flowcharts can help you stay focused on your users and your goals when working on website projects. For example, my website is targeted at any potential employers who might be interested in hiring me for a specific position. By connecting all pages, a user can easily navigate from one page to the next without having to struggle.

Wireframe

A wireframe is a skeletal outline of a webpage in two dimensions. Wireframes show the page structure, layout, information architecture, user flow, functionality, and intended behaviours in detail.
